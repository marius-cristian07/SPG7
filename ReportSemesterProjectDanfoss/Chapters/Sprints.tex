planning, sprint review, and sprint retrospective. Only the most
important ones should appear here; the rest can be referenced from the
Appendix. Each sprint section must include a short summary of the work
done, what was delivered, and relevant reflections. If any conflicts
arose, explain them here and how they were resolved. Each sprint requires
evidence in the form of files documenting the rituals mentioned above.
